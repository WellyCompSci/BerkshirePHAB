About Us

Berkshire Phab is a charity that helps disabled and non-disabled people share experiences, socialise and enjoy activities designed for all abilities. We are open four days a week as a drop-in club for adults, and on Saturdays for children and young people in our youth club. We run occasional outings, and offer a cooked lunch at our weekday club. Berkshire Phab has been in the Reading area for over 40 years and is based in a purpose-built community centre with full disabled access at 22 Mount Pleasant, off Silver street, close to the centre of Reading. We are part of a nationwide network of clubs supported by Phab UK.

Like all Phab clubs, our aim is to promote and encourage opportunities where disabled and non-disabled people can share experiences on an equal basis, working towards an inclusive society. For details of other clubs, visit www.phab.org.uk
