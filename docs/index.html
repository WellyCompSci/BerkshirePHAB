Welcome

Phab inspires and supports children, young people and adults with and without disabilities to make more of life together.
Over 150 Phab Clubs across England and Wales, with 8,000 members, enable children, young people and adults with disabilities to get together with friends and family for all kinds of activities and social events.
Run by local volunteers, Phab Clubs can make a real difference to the lives of people with disabilities and their families too.
